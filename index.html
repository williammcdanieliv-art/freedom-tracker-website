What is the Builder?
The Builder projects your future dividend income from recurring contributions. Set an amount and schedule, pick your funds and a time horizon, and it compounds reinvested distributions month by month to estimate the income your portfolio could produce. Projections use a conservative rate by default and are estimates, not guarantees.
